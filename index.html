Filipe Moreira

Founder, CEO & Experience/Design Director

↗

“The Maestro&Co. strives to make every customers’ experience with your business and digital products meaningful.”

As Experience and Design Director, Filipe ensures Maestro&Co.’s creative vision is expressed across all global projects and leads custom creative teams that operate at the intersection of business and people to experience for global clients.

Filipe, for more than a decade, has been helping brands and companies of all sizes building and improving their business, brands, products and services through people-driven thinking, smart and simple design and interactive experiences. During that time, he has collaborated with global brands like FIFA, Vodafone, IKEA, Nestle, Johnson & Johnson, Canon, Fidelidade, Sagres, Talkdesk, Instapage, Vahdam India, and a bunch of awesome startups and small companies that you probably will hear of in the near future.

His work has been featured in leading publications such as Financial Times, Forbes, TechCrunch, CNBC, The Sunday Times, MIT, Oprah and amongst others.
